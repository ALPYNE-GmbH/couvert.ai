Methodology

Evidence, not opinion.

Every CHF estimate is anchored in three peer-reviewed studies from hospitality research and consumer behavior. No industry guesses, no gut feel.

1 : 26
The silent guests
For every unhappy guest who complains, 26 leave without a word. One written complaint represents 7 silent guests at 1–2★, and 3 at 3★.
Kolsky · ThinkJar field study 2015
5 – 9%
One star of revenue
Each additional rating star lifts revenue at independent restaurants by 5–9%. Causal, not correlational. Chains do not benefit to the same degree.
Luca · Harvard Business School 2016
88% vs 47%
The response effect
88% of consumers are more likely to choose a business that responds to every review, vs. only 47% when the business does not respond. 56% change their mind because of owner responses.
BrightLocal Local Consumer Review Survey 2024

Your AI Operations Team
Four specialized teams. Twelve agents.
Each team solves a concrete problem. The agents share data and decisions, covering the full operational loop from a single review to strategic site optimization.
Team 1

Guest Experience

Reviews. Recovery. Occupancy.

  • Reputation ManagerAnswers every review in four languages and in your tone of voice. Critical cases within 2 hours.
  • Guest RecoveryIdentifies unhappy guests from reviews and reservation data, then brings them back personally.
  • Booking OptimizerSpots occupancy gaps, fills weak slots through recovery invitations, and relieves peak hours.
Team 2

Quality & Operations

Quality. Capacity. Staffing. Communication.

  • Quality WatchdogDetects complaint patterns before they show up in the rating, and briefs the responsible staff before each shift.
  • Operations AdvisorCorrelates reservation data with guest sentiment and adjusts reservation buffers ahead of peak days.
  • Predictive StaffingStaffs by risk, not just by reservations. Combines weather, events, and historical occupancy.
  • Staff CommunicationStructured shift handovers instead of informal chats. Personalized briefings per role.
